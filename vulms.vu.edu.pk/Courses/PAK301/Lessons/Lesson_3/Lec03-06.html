Syed Ahmad Khan’s writings

          Besides his prominent role in the educational uplift of the Muslims, Syed Ahmad Khan’s writings played important role in popularizing the ideals for which the Aligarh stood.

 Risala Asbab-i-Baghawat-i-Hind

His Risala Asbab-i-Baghawat-i-Hind in 1858; He clarified in this book that Muslims were dragged into the war and it is unfair to blame Muslims alone for the war.

Publications of the  Aligarh Movement

 

In this book Sir Sayed Ahmed Khan discussed the Historical buildings.

                Tahzib-ul-Akhlaq                 

This magazine outlined the ethical aspects of the Muslims’ life. Sir Syed Ahmad Khan criticized the conservative way of life of Muslims and advised them to adopt new trends in life. This magazine was written to teach the Muslims to adopt the true teaching of Islam and do not follow the social custom.

Other writings as Loyal Mohammedans of India; Tabyin-ul-Kalam and Khutbat-i-Ahmadiya rooted out the misunderstandings about Islamic teachings and helped create a cordial relation between the British Government and the Indian Muslims and also helped to remove the misunderstanding about Islam and Christianity.
